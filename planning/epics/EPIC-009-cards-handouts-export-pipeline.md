# EPIC-009: Cards, Handouts & Export Pipeline

## Goal

Generate table-ready cards and handouts as projections of entity data, applying templates and visibility rules.

## Scope

- Card templates as JSON.
- Cards as projections, not Ground Truth.
- PDF export.
- PNG export.
- A4 3x3 print sheets.
- Poker, Tarot, A4 sheet, and moderation-card sizes.
- Visibility-aware export.

## Out Of Scope

- Freeform drag-and-drop card designer.
- Pixel-perfect arbitrary template editor.
- AI-assisted card summaries in V1.

## Open Decisions

- Manual card summary requirements.
- Template vocabulary.
- Overflow policy details.
- Export renderer technology.
